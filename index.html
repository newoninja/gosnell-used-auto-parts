Our Story

Three Generations.
One Handshake Promise.

Nestled at the foot of the Blue Ridge Mountains in Flat Rock, NC, Gosnell Used Auto Parts has been a cornerstone of Henderson County's automotive community for over three decades.

Greg Gosnell is the driving force behind everything we do. He built this business on a simple idea: treat every customer like a neighbor, because most of them are. Honest pricing, reliable parts, and straight answers — that's been the formula since day one.

Today, Greg, Rodney, and Dustin run the yard together. Three family members, decades of combined experience, and the same handshake service that built our reputation. You'll always talk to a real person — never an automated system.
